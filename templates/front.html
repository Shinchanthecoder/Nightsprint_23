ABOUT

We are committed to promoting the well-being of every child. Our approach is based on collecting crucial data through periodic assessments, following WHO (World Health Organization) guidelines, and using static forms. These assessments are vital for monitoring a child's growth and development, ensuring that they have the best possible start in life. For children identified as differently-abled, our team offers comprehensive support, including medical consultations, surgeries, therapies (such as physiotherapy, speech therapy, and occupational therapy), assistance in availing social security schemes, enrollment into schools, and provision of adaptive devices. This support is provided through face-to-face interactions or tele-rehabilitation, ensuring that every child receives the care and attention they deserve.
